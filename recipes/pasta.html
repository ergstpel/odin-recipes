Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook the pasta until al dente.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook the garlic.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and simmer for a few minutes.
  5. Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce.
  6. Toss well to coat the pasta evenly.
  7. Serve with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
