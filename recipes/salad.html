Salad

Salad, huh? I have not many words to spend on this wonderful dish, it speaks for itself.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • Tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the vegetables.
  2. Chop the lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, and onion.
  3. Place all the vegetables in a large bowl.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper.
  6. Toss everything together.
  7. Serve immediately.
