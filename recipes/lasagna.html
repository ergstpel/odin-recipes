Lasagna

Lasagna is a beloved dish throughout the whole world.
Therefore I've decided to add it to our menu in order to make customers happy.

Ingredients

  1. Lasagna noodles
  2. Ground beef
  3. Tomato sauce
  4. Ricotta cheese
  5. Mozzarella cheese
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Onion
  8. Garlic
  9. Olive oil
  10. Salt
  11. Pepper

Steps

  • Cook the lasagna noodles.
  • Brown the beef with onion and garlic.
  • Add the tomato sauce and simmer.
  • Mix the ricotta with Parmesan.
  • Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  • Repeat the layers.
  • Top with mozzarella and Parmesan.
  • Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
  • Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
